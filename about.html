Company Profile

During recent years, the laminated product industry in Thailand has witnessed strong and rapid growth. In response to this, Panco invests in the latest technology essential for producing a wide range of quality laminated products.

Background & History

Panco Prosperity Trade Limited was established on March 14, 2001. The registered capital was 10 million baht and machines were imported to manufacture for supply to the local market.

Initially, Panco could produce laminated product 25,000 yards per eight hours per day. Each machine can laminate EVA or sponge with cloths, foam, and coating respectively.

As the demand for laminated product had risen remarkably in line with the country's economic development, the company has grown rapidly, with productivity and sales expanding apace.
